Recent Rishikesh trip

Rishikesh, nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, boasts a unique environment characterized by its location at the confluence of the Ganges River and the Shivalik range. The city is known for its natural beauty, spiritual atmosphere, and efforts to promote a healthy environment.
